Honda Click 150i

With its stylish looks and efficient performance all topped up with an affordable price, the Honda Click 150i is sure to attract many motorcycle buyers of today. Updated in 2018, this scooter is made available with only one Standard version. The said variant retails at P98,900.

Key equipment in the Click 150i includes LED taillights and headlights, a sports-style muffler with aluminum end cap, a digital instrument cluster, and an idling-stop system that switches off the engine during traffic stops, and back on again when it’s time to accelerate in order to avoid unnecessary fuel consumption.

--

Its beating heart is a liquid-cooled, four-stroke, SOHC, 150cc engine that produces 13hp and 13Nm of torque. The engine is fuel-injected and is mated to a continuously variable transmission (CVT). For safety, the Click 150i is fitted with a front disc brake.
